Job Description

Medweb –

Job Description

Summary of Position: We are looking for a talented Senior Software Developer who is passionate about working with cutting edge technology and applying it in a meaningful manner!! This key member of our development team will work closely with the project manager, product owner, CTO and other developers to develop requirements, integrate our software with other systems, products, and build up new functionality to support both synchronous and asynchronous telehealth workflows. Medweb is dedicated to the integration of the most cutting edge technology to the product offerings.

Responsibilities

  • Person will collaborate with the other member of the team to design and deliver solutions in-scope, on-time, and on-budget
  • Develop new features on existing products
  • Actively guide and architect entirely new product suites as necessary
  • Continually look for ways to improve our clients’ technology posture

This position provides an educational and career path for the qualified candidate that will garner them exceptional credentials in the rapidly expanding field of medical imaging and information systems. Medweb also provides substantial opportunity to work on medical humanitarian projects around the world. We provide competitive compensation packages and excellent benefits programs including 401K plan with company matching funds.

Desired Skills & Experience

Requirements:

Bachelor’s Degree in Computing Science or equivalent

Minimum 5 years programming experience in C#/.NET

Minimum 1 year working with Xamarin on iOS and Android

Excellent written and verbal communication skills for technical applications

Ability to adapt and learn new technologies quickly

Must be able to demonstrate legal ability to work in the US

 

Additional Skills We Look For:

Self-motivated, ability to work independently,

Strong problem solving skills

Experience with AR/VR, including Unity

 

Company Description

Started by a physician 20 years ago, Medweb is a technology leader in the delivery of web-enabled, secure healthcare expertise through the application of telecommunications and computer technology.

Medweb's platforms facilitate electronic acquisition, viewing, communication/transmission, publishing, and storage of medical data captured by numerous modalities and devices, and associated with a variety of medical subspecialties including radiology, orthopedics, dermatology, ophthalmology, stroke evaluation, dentistry, and general exams; as well as workflow management.

We work hard, but we love what we do and have fun doing it!

Compensation:

Commiserate with experience

More About Dice Salary Predictions

Where does this estimate come from?

Dice predicts salary ranges based on the job title, location, and skills listed in individual job descriptions. Our proprietary machine-learning algorithm uses more than 600,000 data points to make its predictions. To get the most accurate prediction of the salary you might earn, customize the prediction in your Dice profile. Actual salary offered by employer may vary.

How was this salary estimate calculated?

Dice's predictive salary model is a proprietary machine-learning algorithm. Unlike many other salary tools that require a critical mass of reported salaries for a given combination of job title, location and experience, the Dice model can make accurate predictions on even uncommon combinations of job factors. The model does this by recognizing patterns in the more than 600,000 salary data points to infer how much each factor - job title, location, experience, education, and skills - will impact the salary.

      WHAT ARE COOKIES?



      A cookie is a small file of letters and numbers that we store on your browser or the hard drive of your computer if you agree. Cookies contain information that is transferred to your computer's hard drive.

      WHAT TYPES OF COOKIES DO WE USE?



      We use two types of cookies on our website:

      'Session Cookies'
      These are temporary cookies, which only exist in the period you access the website (or more strictly, until you close the browser after accessing the website). Session cookies help our website remember what you chose on the previous page, therefore avoiding having to re-enter information. On our website, these cookies do not contain personal information, and cannot be used to identify you.

      'Persistent Cookies'
      These are cookies remain on your device after you have visited our website. These cookies help us to identify you as a unique visitor (by storing a randomly generated number).
